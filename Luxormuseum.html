WELCOME TO THE LUXOR MUSEUM

The Luxor Museum of the most prominent regional museums in EgyptIt is located on the Nile Corniche in Luxor (ancient Thebes). It was opened in 1975 and contains rare archaeological artifacts from the temples and tombs of Thebes, distinguished by their excellent presentation and focus on ancient Egyptian art in a historical context, including the "Glory of Thebes" Hall and the "Cache".

  • Main Hall

    The main hall of luxor museum is the cental gallery displaying important ancient Egyptian statues and artifacts .It features famous pharaohs and highlights the artistic achievements of ancient thebes.

  • Akhenaten Hall

    Akhenaten was the first individual in history . He assumed the throne as Pharaoh of Egypt either in 1353 or 1351 BC, ruling for approximately 17 years during the 18th Dynasty of the New Kingdom. Akhenaten is best known to modern scholars for his new religion centered on the god...Aton.

  • Royal Mummies Hall

    As forThe mummies inside the museum , there are four of them: two are on full display, two are wrapped in linen for preservation , and one was purchased by the Niagara Falls Museum in 1800, where it remained for a long time, unknown until it was acquired by the Michael Carlos Museum in Atlanta, USA, in 2006.
